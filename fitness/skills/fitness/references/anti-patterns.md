# Anti-Patterns

Assess for these patterns.

1. Generic Wrapper
- Adds little beyond "use tool X for task Y"

2. Trigger Fog
- Description is broad, vague, or mismatched with actual behavior

3. Tutorial Dump
- Re-explains general concepts instead of encoding reusable knowledge

4. Phantom Capability
- Frontmatter or headings promise behavior the artifact does not support

5. Tool Overreach
- Requests more tools or permissions than the task requires

6. Missing Decision Surface
- No conditional branches for common variation, missing prerequisites, or partial failure

7. Missing Output Contract
- No clear success criteria, deliverable shape, or report format

8. Stale Research Posture
- Claims research capability without source rules, freshness policy, or fallback behavior

9. Dead Bundle
- References examples, scripts, or support files that do not exist or do not help execution

10. Untestable Advice
- Gives high-level advice without concrete prompts, examples, or rewrite moves
